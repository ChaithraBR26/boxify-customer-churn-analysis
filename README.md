# Boxify Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents a Business Analytics case study focused on customer churn analysis for **Boxify**, an e-commerce subscription box service. The objective is to identify factors contributing to customer churn, analyze customer behavior, and recommend data-driven retention strategies to improve customer loyalty and business performance.

## Business Problem

Boxify has experienced an increase in customer churn, with many subscribers cancelling their subscriptions before completing six months. This has resulted in reduced customer lifetime value (CLV), revenue loss, and lower customer retention.

The analysis aims to:

* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Understand factors influencing churn
* Measure the impact of churn on business performance
* Recommend strategies to improve customer retention

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Churn Rate (%)
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Average Engagement Score
* Average Subscription Duration
* Revenue Lost Due to Churn
* Retention by Customer Segment

## Dashboard Insights

The dashboard provides insights into:

* Churn by Age Group
* Churn by Subscription Type
* Churn by Location
* Churn by Payment Mode
* Customer Engagement vs Churn
* Customer Lifetime Value by Churn Status
* Revenue Impact of Customer Churn

## Key Findings

* Customers with lower engagement scores are more likely to churn.
* Monthly subscribers show higher churn rates compared to long-term subscribers.
* Active customers generate significantly higher Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
* Customer churn contributes to substantial revenue loss.
* Customer retention varies across customer segments and locations.

## Recommendations

* Improve customer engagement through personalized campaigns.
* Promote quarterly and annual subscription plans.
* Introduce customer loyalty and rewards programs.
* Implement proactive churn monitoring strategies.
* Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
